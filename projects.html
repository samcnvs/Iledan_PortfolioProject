Minesweeper

The game takes place in a grid-based area known as the map. At the beginning of each game, there are 10 hidden explosives on the map. Players interact with the game by entering coordinates (X, Y) to choose a specific cell on the grid. Players begin the game with three lives. Each interaction with a bomb results in the death of one life and the impacted cell is marked as triggered on the map. The game finishes if a player loses all of his or her life (by hitting three bombs).
